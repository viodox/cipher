Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

CIPHER ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website ciphersignal.xyz. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

CIPHER is designed to work without requiring user accounts or personal information. Here is what we may collect:

  • Email address: Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter (Daily Cipher Drop), submit a contact form, or inquire about advertising. We never collect email addresses without your explicit action.
  • Usage data: We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregate usage data such as page views, visit duration, and general geographic region. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Contract addresses: When you use CipherPot to scan a token, the contract address you enter is sent to third-party APIs (honeypot.is and rugcheck.xyz) for analysis. We do not store these addresses.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you the Daily Cipher Drop newsletter if you subscribe (managed through Kit, formerly ConvertKit)
  • To respond to your contact form submissions (processed through Formspree)
  • To understand general usage patterns and improve our service through anonymous analytics
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Third-Party Services

CIPHER integrates with the following third-party services:

  • CoinGecko API: Provides live cryptocurrency market data. Their privacy policy is available at coingecko.com/en/privacy.
  • Honeypot.is: Provides EVM token scanning for honeypot detection.
  • RugCheck.xyz: Provides Solana token risk analysis.
  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can learn more at Google's advertising policies.
  • Cloudflare: Provides hosting, CDN, and anonymous web analytics.
  • Kit (ConvertKit): Manages our email newsletter subscriptions.
  • Formspree: Processes contact form and advertising inquiry submissions.

Cookies

CIPHER itself does not set any cookies. However, third-party services integrated into our site (specifically Google AdSense and Cloudflare) may use cookies for their own purposes, such as serving relevant ads or providing security features. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Affiliate Links

CIPHER contains affiliate links to cryptocurrency exchanges including Coinbase, Binance US, Kraken, and Crypto.com. When you click these links and sign up, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. These affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed on our website. Affiliate links do not influence our coin analysis, risk ratings, or AI Decrypt reports.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any personal information you provide. Our website is served over HTTPS and hosted on Cloudflare's global network. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

CIPHER is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Request deletion of any personal information we hold about you
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or Google's ad settings

Cryptocurrency investments carry significant risk, including the potential loss of your entire investment. Always do your own research (DYOR) before making any financial decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
